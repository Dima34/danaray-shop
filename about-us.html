Our Story

More than skincare

At DenaRay Essentials, we believe skincare is a way of life and goes further than a daily routine. DenaRay Essentials provides facial and body skincare products that address various skin concerns. Our products are handmade and carefully formulated. We include the finest ingredients to improve skin texture, nourish, and support your skin to help you stay healthy and look better each day.

Achieving self-love through quality skincare

Your relationship with yourself is the most important one you have. Prioritizing your mental health and nourishing your mind, body, and skin can help you be happier and healthier. We advocate appreciating and loving the skin you’re in! Our lineup of skin loving products will cater to your skincare needs, while encouraging you to remember the importance of self-care and self-love.

A message from our founder

“DenaRay Essentials is named in memory of my grandmother, Dena Ray Evans. She taught me selflessness, sentiment, and how to appreciate my beautifully melanated skin. These principles have helped me grow into the woman I am today and reflect everything DenaRay Essentials stands for.”

DaNae’

A black owned skincare company
